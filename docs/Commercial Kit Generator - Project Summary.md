The Commercial Kit Generator is an AI-powered tool that reads your internal project documents and automatically creates nine professional marketing and sales assets in just a few minutes.

- **Reads Your Project Documents:** It starts by analyzing the content you provide, such as technical specifications, business plans, or internal product descriptions.

- **Generates a Complete Commercial Kit:** In minutes, it produces a coordinated set of nine professional documents, ready to be used by sales, marketing, and leadership teams.

### 6. How It Works in 4 Simple Steps

1. **Upload Your Documents:** The user begins by providing their existing project files, such as technical docs or business plans (currently supports `.md` or `.txt` format).
2. **Set Your Target Audience:** Tell the AI who you're speaking to by selecting the ideal customer profile (ICP), target persona (e.g., CEO), tone, and language.
3. **The AI Extracts the Core Message:** The tool's AI reads and understands the documents, identifying the project's key purpose, features, and benefits.
4. **Receive 9 Professional Documents:** Based on this understanding, the tool instantly generates the complete set of nine commercial assets, each tailored for a specific purpose.

The tool uses built-in "recipes" for clear communication, which are explained next.

### 7. The Communication "Checklists" It Uses

The generator uses proven communication frameworks as simple "checklists" to ensure every document is clear, structured, and effective.

- **AIDA:** This is a four-step checklist for persuasive writing: grab **A**ttention, build **I**nterest, create **D**esire, and call to **A**ction.
    - _Example:_ "Tired of slow reports? (Attention) Get them instantly with our tool. (Interest)..."
- **SPIN:** This checklist helps guide a sales conversation by discussing the **S**ituation, identifying the **P**roblem, exploring its **I**mplication, and showing the **N**eed-payoff (the value of your solution).
    - _Example:_ "How long do reports take now? (Situation) Does that delay key decisions? (Problem)..."
- **Message House:** This provides a simple structure for a core message, much like a house: start with one main idea (the 'roof'), support it with three key points (the 'pillars'), and back each point with proof or examples (the 'foundation').
- **C4-inspired clarity:** This is a method for explaining complex systems, perfect for technical documents. It starts with a simple, high-level context and allows you to drill down into details layer by layer, ensuring clarity for both business and technical audiences.

These checklists are used to create the final documents you receive.

### 9. What It Does Not Do (Limitations)

As a showcase project, the tool has a few limitations in its current version:

- It does not yet support uploading PDF or DOCX file types.
- You cannot download all documents in a single ZIP file.
- Advanced features like saving your configurations and rich Markdown previews are planned for a future version.
- As a showcase project, it's intended to demonstrate a concept rather than serve as a production-ready tool.
